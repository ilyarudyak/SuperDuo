About

With this app you can view matches of selected football leagues. You may view matches for Today, two days before and two days after.

What information is available?

You'll get command titles, logos, scores and time of match. If you click on a match you'll get more details: league title and match day.

How to share a match?

From detail view you can use Share button to share information about the match in Twitter, Facebook and other similar apps installed on your device.

Widgets

You may install widgets on a home screen. Two widgets are are available:

  1. small widget that shows the first match for Today;
  2. large collection widget that shows all matches for Today;

Future plans

We are going to add Settings for a user to choose parameters like time frame for matches (currently five days as mentioned about), list of leagues (currently they are predefined) and some parameters for widgets. We also planning to enlarge our collection of command logos.
